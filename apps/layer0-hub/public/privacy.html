Privacy Policy

Antiphon Studios · Last updated: February 2026

What we collect

We collect only what is necessary to provide and improve our products:

  • Account information: Email, display name, and sign-in provider when you create or sign in to an account.
  • Payment information: Processed securely by Stripe. We do not store full payment card details.
  • Anonymous usage data: When you opt in, we collect anonymous, aggregated usage data (e.g. feature usage, error reports) to improve our software. We never track for marketing or personally identify you from this data.

How we use it

We use your information to: provide entitlements, install and update products, process payments, and (when opted in) improve our products via anonymous analytics. We do not sell your data.

Data retention

We retain account and purchase data for as long as your account exists or as required by law. Anonymous usage data is aggregated and retained according to our analytics policy.

Your rights

You may access, correct, or delete your account data. Contact support for assistance. For payments and refunds, see Stripe's policies and our terms.

Contact

For privacy questions: support@antiphonstudios.com (or your preferred contact address).
